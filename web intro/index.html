Face-To-Face Fatigue Detection

During a pandemic almost everyone works from home. According to the journal Ahima and several papers at Researchgate, the time to interact with computers has increased since the pandemic and can lead to fatigue. Fatigue when working from home is caused by too much work and loss of work limits, this has a major impact on health, especially due to the use of too much technology.
This project is designed to solve this problem by using a reminder that can adjust the schedule so that it can restore work boundaries and minimize fatigue due to prolonged use of technology.

How it works?

In the real application, you will see that the app take a picture and start scanning to take a decision if you are in fatigue or not.
